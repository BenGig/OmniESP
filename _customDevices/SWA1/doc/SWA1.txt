--------------
Prepare device
--------------

Use sharp 3 mm flat head screw driver to open device. Unscrew the WiFi board
and solder a 5-pin header to the back of the board.

----------------
Flashing OmniESP
----------------

1. set build_flags in [env:d1_mini] section of platformio.ini as follows:

build_flags = -Wl,-Teagle.flash.2m.ld

Then build firmware.

2. connect USB/Serial/Adapter:

  SWA1  Adapter
  -------------
  RxD   TxD   green
  TxD   RxD   white
  GND   GND   black
  GPIO0 GND
  5V    5V    red  (connect last)

  Sometimes, powering up does not set the bootloader mode. Then just try
  again.

3. Flash OmniESP

4. Flash SPIFFS

5. Done.
